About Us

We are non-for-profit Hope and Healing International at the massive Bath Beach/Bensonhurst Food & Masks Distribution. We gave out food boxes, masks & sanitizers to almost 500 families from all walks of life. We were joined by many hardworking volunteers & community leaders of all ages. Special Thanks to Met Council for providing hundreds of kosher food boxes and to Tashkent Supermarket from Brighton Beach for donating hundreds of fruit packages. Also Big Thanks to Pervez Siddiqui & Marhaba Pharmacy for the donation of masks. And, of course, the success of this event would not be possible without an absolutely amazing team of volunteers from Hope N Healing . We are all in this together!.
